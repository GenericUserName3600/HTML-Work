~ About InTheZone ~

InTheZone is a website and game distribution company that aims to provide high quailty and affordable games straight from the developer themsevles, and thus cutting out the middle man. We are a small team af about 200 employees based in and around london, Uk.

We provide mostly strategy and action games due to those genres being the most popular between our staff and customer base. Some examples of these games would be Stellaris, Dawn Of War 3 and Civilization 5.

As stated above, we are a small team that works in andn around London, Uk. However, we also operate in the US and the Eastern side of the world in order to bring these games and these prices all over the world.
